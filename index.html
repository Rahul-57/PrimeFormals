About PrimeFormals

Your Trusted Partner in Formal Wear Excellence

Since our establishment in 2015, PrimeFormals has been India's premier supplier of high-quality raw materials for formal wear manufacturing. Based in the textile hub of Mumbai, we serve tailors, boutiques, and garment manufacturers across the country.

Our Mission

To provide the finest quality fabrics, threads, buttons, and accessories that enable craftsmen to create exceptional formal wear. We believe that every suit tells a story, and we provide the materials to make that story extraordinary.
